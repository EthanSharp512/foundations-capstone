My application will be a poker companion application to help players/new players play the game and know what hands have value. The application will have 3 features. The first being getting a preflop range at a glance for what position they select. Antoher feature will be a pocket pair calculator where the player can enter a pocket pair and how many opponents they have, and it will tell them their likelihood of winning. The application will also have a tool for the user that recommends how or if they should play the hand. It will let you enter your current seat position, 2 card values and if they are the same suit, then tell the user if they should call, fold, or raise.

Poker Tracker is a poker companion application to use while playing poker to determine odds of winning with certain hands. With this application, you can find out your chances of winning by simply entering in your hand information, and we will give back to you a recommendation determined by your particular circumstances. This web application can give you a recommendation instantly upon receiving the contents of your hand. The application currently includes 3 features. First, a preflop range at a glance where you can see all hands for your current position. Second, a preflop range calculator where a player can enter in their cards, if the cards are suited, and what cards they have to receive a specific recommendation on hand playability. Third, it offers a pocket pair winning odds calculator where you can enter how many player you are playing against and what pocket pair you have, and it will give you a percentage of how often your pocket pair will win against any hand. It is great for new players just learning the game and figuring out how things work.
