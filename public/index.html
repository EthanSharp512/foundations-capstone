Welcome to Poker tracker!

Poker Tracker is a poker companion application to use while playing poker to determine odds of winning with certain hands. With this application, you can find out your chances of winning by simply entering in your hand information, and we will give back to you a recommendation determined by your particular circumstances. This web application can give you a recommendation instantly upon receiving the contents of your hand. The application currently includes 3 features. First, a preflop range at a glance where you can see all hands for your current position. Second, a preflop range calculator where a player can enter in their cards, if the cards are suited, and what cards they have to receive a specific recommendation on hand playability. Third, it offers a pocket pair winning odds calculator where you can enter how many player you are playing against and what pocket pair you have, and it will give you a percentage of how often your pocket pair will win against any hand. It is great for new players just learning the game and figuring out how things work. Give it a try today! Thank you!
